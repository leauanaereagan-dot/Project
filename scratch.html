Why Workout Splits Matter

Choosing the right workout split helps maximize recovery, muscle growth, and consistency.

Popular Workout Splits

  1. Push/Pull/Legs (PPL)
    • Push: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
    • Pull: Back, Biceps
    • Legs: Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes
  2. Upper/Lower Split
    • Upper: Chest, Back, Shoulders, Arms
    • Lower: Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves
  3. Bro Split (NEVER DO)
    • Day 1: Chest
    • Day 2: Back
    • Day 3: Shoulders
    • Day 4: Arms
    • Day 5: Legs
